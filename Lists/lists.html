Angela's Recipe for the best Cinamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons groiund cinamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 mins
  5. Treansfer the dough into a large bowl and cover the plastic wrap
